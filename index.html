What is User Generated Content (UGC)

User-generated content is when your customers create videos, images or reviews about your product or service on websites and social media. For example, someone might post a picture on Instagram or write a blog post.

Understand how your customers think and feel at scale.

Record or upload videos

No need to download any apps, customers simply head to your business profile and start recording or upload their video.

Review submitted UGC

Set upload prerequisites, get instant notification, analysis, automatic moderation and understand customer sentiment.

Convert more customers

Share submitted UGC reviews on your site or across social media. Use social proof as marketing to attract more sales.
