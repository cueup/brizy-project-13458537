Frequently asked questions

Can anyone become a viviews™ creator?

If you are already an influencer or content creator, you're good to go. You must be at least 18 years old and be able to make great videos.

How do I get paid?

You get paid as soon as your viview (video) is approved, if the viview gig is cash based. Otherwise, you'll be compensated by being able to keep the product or service you review.

Can I choose the brand I work with?

Sure! You can choose to approach a brand or brands might approach you. Either way, you be able to review the requirements and make a decision who you work with.

How will I receive product to review?

Products are securely shipped to you free or charge, in most cases you'll get to keep these products and in others you'll be paid a monetary sum.

Do I have to post videos online?

No, you'll only need to upload the videos on viviews.

Can I use my phone to post videos?

Of course, you can start with a phone or if your more experienced feel free to use professional equipment to show off your skills and take your submissions to the next level.
